About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an adrenaline-pumping roguelite survival game where you must fend off endless waves of monsters with evolving abilities and strategic upgrades.

Inspired by popular titles like Vampire Survivors, this game challenges you to survive as long as possible while collecting experience, unlocking new characters, and discovering powerful synergies between different weapons and abilities.

Each run is unique, with procedurally generated enemy patterns and random upgrade options that force you to adapt your strategy on the fly. How long can you survive the monster apocalypse?

How to Play

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • Mouse: Aim certain weapons (if applicable)
  • Space: Use special ability (when charged)
  • ESC: Pause game

Gameplay Cycle

  1. Survive waves of monsters
  2. Collect experience gems from defeated enemies
  3. Level up and choose new weapons or upgrades
  4. Find treasure chests for rare items
  5. Defeat bosses to unlock new content
  6. Eventually succumb to the horde (or triumph!)
