About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an addictive survival game where you face endless waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Your goal is simple - survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups, upgrading your character, and defeating epic bosses.

With intuitive controls and fast-paced gameplay, Monster Survivors offers a thrilling experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat. How long can you survive in this monster-infested world?

Perfect for quick gaming sessions or extended play, Monster Survivors features procedurally generated levels, ensuring that no two games are exactly alike.
